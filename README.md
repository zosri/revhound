# RevHound

**Your real net revenue, after everything.**

RevHound shows developers what they actually keep after platform fees, VAT, FX losses, and payment processing — across Google Play, App Store, Stripe, and Shopify.

## The Problem

You sold an app for €8.84. After VAT (23%), platform fees (15%), FX losses (~2%), and payment processing, you actually made €5.01. That's 56.7% of what the dashboard told you.

Every platform shows you gross revenue. Nobody shows you the real number. RevHound does.
